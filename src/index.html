About Us

Pathfinders Internship and Leadership Programs

by HINDUACTion

HINDUACTion is looking for energetic and outgoing American Hindu high school or college students. We offer two distinct internship opportunities for students to develop leadership skills, gain experience, and engage with their communities.

These programs offer a unique opportunity to make a tangible impact while equipping yourself with the tools, experience, and connections needed to thrive in public service or any leadership role.

  • 01

    PATHFINDERS American Hindu Congressional Internship

    Open to undergraduates, and graduate students of any major.

  • 02

    PATHFINDERS American Hindu State Internship

    Open to high school and undergraduate students

Program Highlights

Why Choose Pathfinders

Take the next step toward shaping your future and your community!

  • Gain experience

    An immersive internship in state offices for hands-on experience and connecting with high-level policymakers and insiders.

  • Workshop

    Comprehensive program orientation as well as tailored professional development workshops on leadership and policy.

  • Networking

    Exclusive networking opportunities and access to mentorship throughout the program.

  • Placement support

    Ongoing support for job or internship placements after program completion.

  • Stipend

    A competitive stipend is offered for the duration of the Congressional internship.*

  • Trip To D.C.

    Take an exciting trip to Washington, D.C., and experience public office firsthand through a Congressional internship.

Eligibility

PATHFINDERS American Hindu Congressional Internship

  • 01

    Enrolled in University

    Enrolled at an accredited university in the U.S. or graduated within the last year, with GPA > 3.0.

  • 02

    Interest

    A demonstrated interest in politics, government, American Hindu history, or related fields.

  • 03

    In-person internship

    Work a minimum of 20 hours a week on congressional office assignments.

  • 04

    Attend Training

    Attend a 10-day orientation, weekly leadership training sessions, and complete the course.

  • 05

    Adhere to policies

    Comply with HinduACTion policies regarding work performance and personal conduct.

  • 06

    US Citizen

    Must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident.
