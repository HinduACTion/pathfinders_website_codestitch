About Us

Pathfinders Internship and Leadership Initiative

by HINDUACTion

HINDUACTion is looking for energetic and outgoing American Hindu High School or College students. We offer two distinct internship opportunities for students to learn about and present a unique civilizational paradigm, develop leadership skills and gain hands-on public policy experience at both the State and Federal levels.

These programs provide a chance to make meaningful impact while equipping participants with the educational tools, leadership experience, and valuable networks to excel in public service or any leadership role.

After participating in the leadership initiatives, College students may have the opportunity to intern with Congressional Representatives. High School students may be able to intern with their State or Local Representatives.

  • 01

    PATHFINDERS American Hindu Congressional Internship

    Open to Undergraduates and Graduate students of any major (18+)

  • 02

    PATHFINDERS American Hindu State Internship

    Open to High School and Undergraduate students (16+)

Program Highlights

Why Choose Pathfinders

Shape the future of the world!

  • Unique effort in Education

    In partnership with Hindu University of America (HUA) and prominent scholars, situate the development of Hindu thought in its historical as well as socio-political context & contrast them with some of the axiomatic ideas originating from other religions & ideologies.

  • Leadership Training

    Learn different aspects of media, public policy making & their implementation from subject matter experts & policy makers.

  • Networking

    Exclusive networking opportunities with influential thought leaders & policy makers in Washington D.C.

  • Placement support

    Ongoing support for internship placement after successful completion of free educational & leadership training.

  • Stipend

    Earn a generous stipend from HINDUACTion during the internship. Earn up to $4000 from HINDUACTion on top of your Congressional stipend.*

  • Immersion Program in Washington D.C.

    Take a fully paid trip to Washington D.C., and meet and learn from elected officials. Attend 20 hours of in-person training during winter 2026 for students 18 and older. Students under 18 at the time of travel have the option to join the 20-hour training online from their respective locations.

Eligibility

Pathfinders American Hindu Congressional Internship

  • 01

    Enrolled in University

    Enrolled at an accredited university or college in the U.S. or graduated within the last year, with GPA > 3.0.

  • 02

    Interest

    A demonstrated interest in politics, governance, history of Hindu civilization & related fields.

  • 03

    Attend Training

    Attend a 50-hour training program: 25 hours of educational and 25 hours of leadership training. Internship assistance to be provided after the successful completion of the program.

  • 04

    Adhere to policies

    HinduACTion Pathfinders Program Policies and Code of Conduct To Be Signed at the Time of Onboarding.

  • 05

    U.S. Citizen

    Must be a U.S. citizen or Permanent Resident and over 18 years old.
